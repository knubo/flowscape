Gameplay

Help the flow reach something yellow as fast as possible. It's normally in the bottom. Click to remove or add part of the labyrinth to provide safe and fast passage. Try to make it in as few ticks as possible!

Share your score

Share the game with a friend. From the score page, click on a score and then share. Send the picture with your score to a friend that has the same model of IOS as you are playing with. The friend can import the score by enabling "Flow import" on the picture itself. The score will then be loaded to your lists of score, but presented in organge. Try to beat the score and send the score back again! You can also import a score using your camera by clicking the import score from the scores page.

Devices

The gameplay for FlowMaze will differ based on the screen size of your iOS device. Be sure to play it on both your iPad and iPhone!
